Everyday Wellness

Foundations for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Nutrition

Focusing on whole foods, balanced macronutrients, and sufficient vitamin intake to support daily metabolic function and long-term health.

Sleep

Prioritizing 7-9 hours of quality sleep to facilitate cognitive recovery, hormone regulation, and immune system strengthening.

Movement

Incorporating both cardiovascular exercise and strength training to maintain joint health, bone density, and heart function.

Mental Wellbeing

Managing stress through mindfulness, healthy boundaries, and social connection to reduce cortisol levels and prevent burnout.

Hydration

Maintaining adequate fluid intake to support cellular function, digestion, and temperature regulation throughout the day.

Preventive Care

Staying proactive with regular check-ups, screenings, and hygiene practices to catch potential health issues early.
